Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Introduction

EverydayAmazingEarth ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the Service:

  • Authentication data: When connecting via TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram, we receive OAuth tokens to publish content on your behalf. We do not access your personal profile data beyond what is required for posting.
  • Usage data: Basic analytics about video performance (views, likes) provided by social media platform APIs.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information from viewers of our content.

4. How We Use Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Publish educational video content to connected social media accounts
  • Monitor content performance for quality improvement

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties. Content is published through official platform APIs (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram) in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Storage and Security

Authentication tokens are stored securely using environment variables and encrypted storage. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect stored data.

7. Data Retention

We retain authentication tokens only as long as necessary to provide the Service. You may revoke access at any time through your social media platform settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke the app's access to your social media accounts at any time
  • Request deletion of any data we hold about you
  • Request information about what data we have collected

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
